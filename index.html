How to play

Super Tic Tac Toe is a larger, more intelligent version of Tic Tac Toe. The board has 9 big squares, and each one is its own mini-board. The cell you choose in a mini-board decides the big board where your opponent must play. If that big board is full or already won, they can play anywhere. Win 3 big boards in a row (across, down, or diagonal) to win the game.
