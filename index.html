How to play

Super Tic Tac Toe (also called Ultimate Tic Tac Toe) is an advanced version of the classic XO game. The board has 9 big squares (3×3), and each big square is itself a small XO game (with 9 cells). The first player (say X) can play in any small square. The position chosen inside the small board decides the big board where the opponent must play next. Example: If you pick the top-left cell inside a small board → your opponent must play in the top-left big board. If the target big board is already full or finished, the next player can play anywhere. The goal: win 3 big boards in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally), just like classic XO.
